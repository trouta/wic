Our Mission

Women in Computing was created in order to provide a community for women pursuing a future in the technology discipline. Historically, women have been the minority in STEM fields, but more and more women are choosing this path. We exist to provide resources and opportunities to this population at Grand Valley State University, supporting the professional endeavors of students, while also strenthening our social network of female peers.
